Featured Destinations

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, an ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, is a monument to love. Built by emperor Shah Jahan for his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, it's a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a dazzling example of Mughal architecture.

Rajasthan

Nicknamed the "Land of Kings," Rajasthan in northwestern India stuns with majestic forts and palaces. This vibrant state also boasts the Thar Desert's vastness and the beauty of Udaipur's serene lakes.

Goa

Goa, India's smallest state, charms with its sun-kissed beaches, serene backwaters, and vibrant Portuguese colonial architecture. Lush greenery and delicious seafood add to Goa's laid-back vibe, making it a perfect beach paradise.

Kerala

Kerala, on India's southwestern coast, is a land of enchanting beauty. Lush green hills unfurl towards serene backwaters and palm-fringed beaches. This state is also renowned for Ayurveda, a traditional healing system, and its rich cultural heritage.
